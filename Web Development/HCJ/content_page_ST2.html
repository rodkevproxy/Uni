Why Do Energywaste Matter?

Energy waste is a massive global bottleneck with severe consequences, being the most impactful one the climate change and emisions. Most of the energy in the world still comes from fossil fuels meaning that every single waste of energy leads into pumping unnecessary greenhouse gases

Wasted energy costs money, energy ineficiency costs consurmers and businesses tons of money, which contributes to a leak in the economy systems of any organisation.

Current Threats To The Energywaste

Many places all over the world are operating on electrical grid that were built decades ago, these grids experience massive leaks of energy when the energy is needed to travel long distances

Data centers are experimenting an incredible incrementation during the past years, the increasing demand of AI systems every day is forcing companies to increase the structures dedicated to the generation of huge amounts of energy.

How You Can Help

  • Document The Data Of The Enery Consumed In Your Household.

Conclusion

Energy waste is one of the invisible leaks that are currently present in the global economy, systematic changes and transitioning into renewable grids and infrastructure are required to reduce the gap that slowly gets bigger, behavioral changes at this point are just critical.
